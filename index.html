Welcome to Our Page

We are committed to ornithology; both as a pursuit and a means of studying the winged creatures who inhabit our world. Our site contains a great deal of information about many species; their habitats, migratory patterns and distribution, and links to their threat status per the WWF.

We also have data/aispeed velocity on the unladed swallow; both European and African.

So, don't waste time! Get out and get what you need! Turn off that computer and get out into the great outdoors!
